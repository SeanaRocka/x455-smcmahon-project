About Brochet & Friends

We are A Magazine for Bro’s, Hookers & Yarn Lovers!

This Magazine is for anyone who uses a crochet hook (i.e. A Hooker.) We are yarn enthusiasts, Bros and people who love to challenge stereotypes and get crafty. We are for Crochet Lovers and Knitting enthusiasts. We also love colors and RAINBOWS, fashion, glyphys and polka dots! This Magazine is for all lovers of yarn and fun. This issue is the culmination of a great deal of hooking and organizing. Our mission is to bring love and peace to the world through yarn. We love you, we love crochet, we love Bros who crochet and anyone who loves yarn.

While crocheting out in the world is fun, it is important to be prepared. Have all of your supplies, and also to pay attention to your own safety. Some people may think that you are knitting, but they are ignorant to the art of Crochet!!

There are many easy crochet projects! Pick something like this poncho, or a scarf and get started on your first crochet project.

To crochet bomb, all you need is yarn and a location. You can yarn bomb your mailbox, or the pole for a street sigh, or even an entire bus!
